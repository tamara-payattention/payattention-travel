How it works

Payment moves from the click to the completed booking.

A traveler hits a subscription paywall in a partner app and rejects the price.

Our offer: “Upgrade your seat — and your subscription is covered for the trip.”

The traveler completes a real booking in your existing flow — seat upgrade, room upgrade, or e-SIM. We build no booking rails and never touch the payment.

You verify on your side: booking completed → paid → not refunded. Attribution by unique code, with traveler consent.

You pay per verified booking. Cancelled or refunded → no charge.

Cohort quality

Why this brings bookers, not coupon-hunters.

Same spend, different psychology. Covering a subscription pulls travelers who were going to book anyway — not people hunting a discount code.

typical promo
“€20 off your booking”

Attracts anyone who wants €20 off. They’d have booked cheaper elsewhere, or not at all. Worse cohort, same cost.

our offer
“Your subscription, covered for the trip”

Feels bigger than a coupon (mental accounting). Costs you only when a real, paid booking completes.

Public discount codes

No open-internet codes like “TRAVEL20”. We never run them — they leak, get farmed, and erode your fares.

Closed partner flows only

The offer appears only inside partner apps, behind a paywall. You approve every traffic partner and every screen where your brand appears.

FAQ

The questions you’re about to ask

Can travelers fake the booking?
You define what counts — completed, paid, not refunded. Your booking engine, your rules. We accept your definition of “verified”.
Are these travelers worth anything to us?
The verified action is a paid booking — it self-selects real travelers. Minimum booking value is set by you.
Where does your traffic come from?
Partner apps only, behind paywalls. You approve every traffic partner and every screen where your brand appears.
How do you prove these bookings aren’t organic?
Your flow opened with our attribution code + booking confirmed within 24h — both events on your side. Organic browsers don’t do that inside the window. Outside it, we don’t bill.
What about refunds and cancellations?
Not billed. We only charge on bookings that complete, pay, and hold past the refund window.
What data do you need from us?
Booking confirmed/refunded per attribution code. No traveler-level data access.
Who pays for the covered subscription?
We do, out of the per-booking fee, via our payment rail. No cash ever reaches the traveler.
